Portal

Containerized identity and resource management for HPC clusters

Architected and deployed a containerized, multi-tenant HPC platform (the CLOACK Stack) integrating allocation management (ColdFront), federated identity (Keycloak), and workload scheduling (Slurm) to serve both the university and external research partners from a single secure deployment. The platform also includes an integrated helpdesk with an AI-driven support engine — routing requests across local and cloud LLMs with retrieval-augmented context — to accelerate researcher support and automate routine operational workflows.
